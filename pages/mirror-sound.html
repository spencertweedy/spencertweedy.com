Mirror Sound is a book about making records
in your bedroom
at your kitchen table
from a car
in your backyard
It’s a book about the special sound of music written and recorded by just one person
(or one + loved ones)
about making what you want to make, with the tools that are available to you
about giving yourself permission
escaping present reality
and inventing something new
