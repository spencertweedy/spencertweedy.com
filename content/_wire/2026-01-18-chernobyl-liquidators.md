The medal that the Soviet Union awarded to servicepeople who contained and cleaned up the Chernobyl disaster [depicts alpha and beta particles and gamma rays over a drop of blood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chernobyl_liquidators).

Clean-up of the site is scheduled to be completed [by 2065](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chernobyl_disaster).
